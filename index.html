Engagement 8PM onwards

The engagement ceremony is one of the main highlights of an Indian wedding ceremony. It is a fun-filled event held at the groom’s place in the attendance of relatives and close friends from both sides. This special occasion is also the moment to enjoy the excitement of exchanging gifts between the two families.

Haldi 6AM - 8AM

The haldi ceremony is a pre-wedding ritual in Indian culture that involves applying turmeric paste to the bride and groom. The ceremony is usually held on early morning of the Wedding Day, and the couple and their close relatives and friends participate.
